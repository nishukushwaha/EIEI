The Education Institute for Early Intervention (EIEI)

We reshape learning by fostering inclusivity and equipping community programs, professionals, and families with the necessary support, resources, training and development to uplift every child with diverse abilities, setting the highest standard in special education, early childhood development, and life-time success!

Empowering Minds, Nurturing Hearts

Early Intervention Services

Free to low-cost on-site special education social playgroups and classrooms for children ages 3-5, supported by Certified Educators, Special Needs Instructors, RBTs, and Occupational and Speech Therapy support.

Training & Professional Development

Empowering owners, directors, and educators to expand their knowledge of special education and its legal aspects concerning the children and families they serve.

Community Programs

Supporting 20+ Early Childhood Education programs with training, coaching, professional development, and fostering family engagement through Pre-K Counts partnership.

Family Empowerment

Join our vibrant online community and Family Empowerment Corner Sessions where families and educators support one another.

Supporting Communities

We join hands with non-profit and for-profit partners, aligning our efforts to support their mission through financial resources and collaborative efforts.

CACFP Sponsorship

As a sponsoring organization of the Child and Adult Care Food Program, we offer vital administrative support ensuring child and adult care providers deliver quality care and nutritious meals.
