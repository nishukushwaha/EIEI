Our Services & Programs

Comprehensive early intervention and special education services designed to empower children, families, and communities.

Ages 3-5

Early Intervention Services & Behavioral Health

EIEI offers free to low-cost on-site special education social playgroups and classrooms tailored for children ages 3-5, supported by Certified Educators, Special Needs Instructors, Registered Behavior Technicians (RBT), and Occupational and Speech Therapy support. Our agency also offers comprehensive in-school and in-home services, ensuring that your child receives the best care and support they deserve!

Learn More About This Program →

Training, Coaching, and Professional Development

Empowering owners, directors, and educators to expand their knowledge of special education and its legal aspects concerning the children and families they passionately serve. Our professional development programs are designed to build capacity within early childhood education programs and schools.

Learn More About This Program →

Community Programs & Partnerships

We go further together! EIEI has proudly been awarded a 5-year grant in partnership with Pennsylvania's Pre-K Counts. Through this initiative, we support 20+ remarkable Early Childhood Education (ECE) programs with training, coaching, professional development, classroom management, fostering family engagement, supporting the growth of children with diverse abilities, and more!

Family Empowerment & Support

Join our vibrant online community and Family Empowerment Corner Sessions where families and educators come together to support one another, ranging from tips on self-care to navigating city and state special education services.

Supporting Communities & Partnerships

CACFP Sponsorship & Administrative Support

As a sponsoring organization of the Child and Adult Care Food Program (CACFP), EIEI offers vital administrative support and a peer-to-peer network ensuring child and adult care providers can deliver quality care and nutritious meals.
