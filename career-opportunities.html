Current Openings

Special Education Teachers

$48,000 - $49,000 annually

We're searching for passionate individuals who possess the skills to make a lasting impact. As a certified teacher, you'll help children with developmental delays or disabilities achieve their full potential.

  • Lead classroom instruction and activities
  • Develop individualized education plans (IEPs)
  • Collaborate with therapists and support staff
  • Engage with families and caregivers

Special Education Teaching Assistants

$15 - $17 per hour

Inspire and educate with full training and support. No prior teaching experience required!

  • Support classroom instruction
  • Assist with student care and activities
  • Help implement behavior strategies
  • Full training provided

Registered Behavior Technicians (RBT)

$28,000 - $30,000 annually

Make a significant difference in young lives. Our RBT role offers a unique opportunity with a focus on evidence-based strategies and collaborative approaches.

  • Implement behavior intervention plans
  • Collect and analyze behavioral data
  • Provide direct student support
  • Collaborate with BCBA and staff

Speech Language Therapists

$88 - $93 per hour

Guide children towards effective communication skills, enabling them to express themselves and connect with the world around them.

  • Assess communication needs
  • Develop and implement therapy plans
  • Provide individual and group therapy
  • Collaborate with educational team

Occupational Therapists - Early Childhood (Ages 3-5)

$86 - $92 per hour

Occupational therapists are crucial for supporting children with developmental delays or disabilities. Utilize play-based interventions to develop foundational skills necessary for learning and daily living.

  • Assess occupational therapy needs
  • Design play-based interventions
  • Support fine and gross motor development
  • Provide classroom and in-home services
